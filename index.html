Who’s Building the Future?

Mapping the Global Landscape of AI Model Development

This project explores the distribution of AI models across domains, organizations, and countries. It visualizes patterns in accessibility and highlights key players building today's most notable models.

The data is sourced from Epoch AI's database of notable machine learning models — selected based on citation count, benchmark performance, historic relevance, and usage.

A 360° View of AI Strength

What the Axes Represent

Talent: Availability of skilled practitioners for AI.
Infrastructure: Access to computing, electricity, internet.
Operating Environment: Public support and regulations for AI.
Research: Research activity in AI journals.
Development: Core platform and algorithm development.
Government Strategy: National-level policy and investment.
Commercial: Startups and market-based AI innovation.
Notable Models: Number of significant AI models developed.
Publications: Academic publishing volume.
Investment (USD Billion): Financial investment in AI.

About This Project

This dashboard visualizes the global development of influential AI models. It highlights key domains, organizations, and countries contributing to AI innovation and maps their model accessibility strategies.

Dataset: Focused on notable ML models (state-of-the-art, cited 1000+, historical, high usage). Compiled from Papers with Code, literature, and expert reviews.

Source: Epoch AI, epochai.org/data/notable-ai-models. Accessed Oct 2024.
